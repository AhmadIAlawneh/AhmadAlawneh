ISCOSOFT

Esraa Company for Programming and Computer was established in 1997 in Nablus as a company specialized in the production of advanced and developed software at the level of the state and the Arab world. The Esraa team currently includes more than 50 employees distributed over the following main departments (research and development, follow-up, operation, marketing, sales, finance, audit and quality). In addition to the company's headquarters in the city of Nablus, the company's branch was opened in the city of Ramallah in 2004 to serve and follow up customers in the central and southern regions. The company is proud to provide its services and products to more than 7,000 customers in Palestine, Saudi Arabia, Jordan and some other countries.
